Package-overview

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the enchanting island of Bali with our meticulously crafted Bali tour package. Begin your adventure in the vibrant city of Kuta, unwinding at the hotel or soaking up the sun at the nearest beach. Indulge in retail therapy at Discovery Mall and experience Bali's famed nightlife at Rock Bar. Discover the beauty of Kintamani Volcano and the artistic villages of Tohpati, Celuk, and Mas. Marvel at Mount & Lake Batur's stunning vistas and savor exotic coffees at a local plantation. Visit the iconic Tanah Lot Temple, perched atop a dramatic rock formation overlooking the Indian Ocean, and immerse yourself in Balinese culture. Experience adrenaline-pumping water sports at Tanjung Benoa beach, enjoying banana boat rides, jet skiing, and parasailing for an exhilarating day. Indulge in a variety of activities in Seminyak, from exploring Bali Safari and Marine Park to unwinding at Potato Head Beach Club or diving into vibrant nightlife.

Itinerary

Day 1

Arrive Indonesia - Bali:

Welcome to Bali – Land of dreams! Upon arrival at Denpasar Airport, you will be met by a representative who will assist with the transfer to your hotel in Bali. Check-in and relax. Remaining time is at leisure to explore the island on your own. Bali, an island of Indonesia, is so beautiful it will leave you speechless.

Day 2

Half Day Water Sports:

Today enjoy breakfast at the hotel. Later you will be transferred to the beach at Tanjung Benoa near Nusa Dua Bali. Here you will enjoy the water sport activities. The activities included are 1 Jet Ski ride & 1 Banana Boat ride. Salty Air, Sandy Hair! Feel the tropical breeze and get closer to Indian Ocean with your selected Water Sport Activity!

Day 3

Bali: Full Day Kintamani Tour with Hot Springs followed with Ubud Coffee Plantation & Ubud Royal Palace:

Enjoy breakfast in the hotel. Today morning proceed for the Kintamani Village.Kintamani Village is a name of countryside located in the hill. You can see beautiful panorama from Kintamani Village to overview the active volcano of Batur Mount and wide Lake of Batur in a valley. It is situated in the plateau area with cool weather surrounding it.

Day 4

Day Free:

Post breakfast at hotel, the day is Free at Leisure.

Day 5

Depart Bali:

This morning after breakfast, you will be transferred to the airport for your onward flight home. Important Note: The itinerary is tentative and subjected to change without prior notice. Final Itinerary will be handed over on the day of arrival.

How many days are enough in Bali?

According to locals and frequent travellers, a weekend (or 3 days) is enough to explore the island of Bali if you aim to view the destination through its attraction and take a small hike. If you are planning to indulge in the local culture, and spend more time enjoying the beaches and ocean, 6-7 days are recommended.
